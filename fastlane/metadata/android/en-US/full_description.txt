Offlock is a zero-knowledge password manager: everything is encrypted on your device with AES-256-GCM and an Argon2id-derived key. No accounts, no servers, no telemetry.

Features:

• System passkey provider (Android 14+)
• Autofill for apps and browsers with strict domain matching (anti-phishing) and saving of new credentials
• 2FA codes (TOTP) with QR scanning, auto-copied when autofilling
• Password and passphrase generator with strength meter
• Audit: weak, reused, old and breached passwords (Have I Been Pwned with k-anonymity)
• Cards, secure notes, identities and encrypted file attachments
• Multiple independent vaults, categories, tags and favorites
• Import from KeePass (.kdbx), Chrome, Bitwarden and LastPass (CSV)
• Encrypted backups, auto-backup to a folder and rotating local backups
• Biometric unlock, auto-lock, clipboard clearing and screenshot blocking
• Password history and a trash with 30-day recovery
